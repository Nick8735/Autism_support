Parenting is a challenging responsibility, and comprehending your child's needs can feel overwhelming. Moreover, locating the necessary information can be akin to finding a needle in a haystack.

Support With Autism offers an open feed forum where all parents can share their experiences, whether positive or negative, providing a platform for mutual support and exchange.

There is still much we have yet to discover about Autism, and parents continue to discover new support mechanisms on a daily basis. Our biggest challenge lies in the unknown, and the most significant and effective tool we have is sharing information.

NOTE: Please consult your doctor before attempting to implement any new approaches. This platform does not have medical professionals available.

It is crucial to always be aware of the risks involved concerning your child's well-being.
